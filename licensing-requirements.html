LICENSING REQUIREMENTS

Becoming a successful real estate agent requires a commitment of time, money and pre-licensing education.

Get started with your real estate career

1.Sales associate prerequisites:
  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • hold a high school diploma or it's equivalent
  • Have a United States Social Security number
2.Complete pre-licensing aducation. Successfully complete a Florida Real Estate Commission-approved pre-licensing course for sales associates consisting of 63 classroom hours
3.Register fingerprints, An applicant must have a background check as part of the licensing process
4.Apply for license. Submit a completed application to FREC. This application must include electronic fingerprints and appropiate fees to FREC
5.Schedule and pass the exam. Pass the Florida Real Estate Sales Associate Examination.
6.Acquire broker affiliation or activate your license. A sales associate’s real estate license is not technically active until he/she is affiliated with a licensed broker. A sponsoring broker can activate your license using the DBPR RE 10–Sales Associate form.

Where to take classes and get more information

Unfortunately, there’s no one database for accredited real estate schools in Florida. Try Google or another search engine. You won’t be disappointed.
